Ischia, Italy

Ischia is a island in Italy that is known as The Green Island. It is a popular tourist destination due to its beautiful landscape and attractions!


Giardini Ravino

Giardini Ravino, is a park surrounded by one of the biggest collections of succulent plants in Europe. It is a popular tourist attraction and not only can u find beautiful plants here, but peacocks as well!

Via Provinciale Panza, 140b, 80075 Forio NA, Italy


Guevara Tower

Guevara Tower has a legend that Renaissance artist, Michelangelo, once lived in the tower. It is a emblem of Ischia, and due to this, it is a popular tourist attraction!

Via Nuova Cartaromana, 80077 Ischia NA, Italy


Aragonese Castle

Aragonese Castle is a castle on a small Phelgraean island. It is a medieval castle with beautiful architechure and a lot of history, due to this, it is a hotspot for tourists!

Castello Aragonese, 80077 Ischia NA, Italy
